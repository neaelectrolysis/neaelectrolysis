Welcome to NEA Electrolysis. These Terms & Conditions govern your use of our services and our website. By scheduling an appointment, you agree to these Terms & Conditions in full. If you disagree with any part of these terms and conditions, please do not use our services.



NEA Electrolysis provides professional electrolysis for hair removal services. All services are performed by a trained, licensed electrologist. We are available by appointment only, no walk-ins accepted.We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone for any reason at any time.



To streamline scheduling and reduce no-shows, a valid debit or credit card is required on file to secure all appointments. No card on file = no appointment. By scheduling with NEA Electrolysis, you agree to this policy.



Payment is due at the time of service. We accept cash, credit cards, and Venmo. Prices for services are subject to change without notice.



Appointment confirmations are appreciated but not required. Failure to confirm does not cancel the appointment. All policies above still apply.



Consultations are complimentary and include 15-30 minutes of electrolysis; depending on the treatment area. This helps give you a taste of what electrolysis feels like, how your skin will look after treatment and how it will recover, so you can make an informed decision before agreeing to a treatment plan.

We require a 48-hour notice to cancel or reschedule a consultation. Failure to cancel or reschedule without 48-hour notice will result in a $25 charge to the card on file.

Online consultation forms (New Client Intake & Health History) must be completed at least 24 hours before your consultation. If you prefer to complete paper forms only, please contact us as soon as possible at (262) 227-2576 so we can prepare to accommodate that request.

Links to our Pre & Post Treatment Instructions, Informed Consent form, and Terms & Conditions will also be sent by email. We highly recommend viewing these documents BEFORE your consultation.

Clients who have not received a service within the past 6 months must first contact us and re-submit all consultation forms before scheduling an appointment. This is to keep your information up-to-date and ensure safe treatment.



A 50% deposit is required to schedule any appointment and will be applied toward the total cost of the service. If a client cancels more than 48 hours before their appointment, and chooses not to reschedule, the deposit will be refunded.

The deposit will be forfeited if any of the following occur:
• Late cancellation
• Late rescheduling
• Missed appointment (no show)
• Arriving with less than 15 minutes remaining in the scheduled time slot
• Service discontinued due to misconduct

NEA Electrolysis LLC reserves the right to deny future appointments to clients with repeated:
• Late cancellations
• Late rescheduling
• Late arrivals
• Missed appointments
• Unpaid fees

If a client chooses to end their appointment early due to discomfort or any other reason, the cost of the service will be whichever is greater:

A) The required deposit for the original appointment length, or
B) The actual time used, billed in 15-minute increments (time >7 min will be rounded up to 15 min).

Example:
A client schedules a 60-minute appointment but ends the service at 38 minutes. The client will be charged for three 15-minute increments, totaling $63.75 before tax.



We understand that unexpected situations occur. However, last-minute changes make it difficult to offer the appointment time to other clients. Many clients must grow out their hair before electrolysis, so filling cancellations on short notice is challenging. For this reason, 48 hours’ notice is required to cancel or reschedule any appointment. Appointments canceled or rescheduled without 48 hours’ notice will forfeit the deposit.



If the client arrives late with more than 15 minutes remaining for their scheduled appointment time, the service will be provided but not extended beyond the scheduled time slot.

If the client has not arrived once 15 minutes or less is remaining for their scheduled time slot, the appointment will be canceled and service will not be provided.

In these two events, along with missed appointments (ie “no shows”), the client will be responsible for the full cost of the originally scheduled appointment. The card on file will be charged accordingly. Any outstanding balance must be paid in full before scheduling a new appointment.

ONE-TIME COURTESY EXCEPTION
As a courtesy, clients are allowed one late cancellation, late rescheduling, or missed appointment (if we are notified at least 30 minutes in advance) per 12-month period without penalty. This one-time courtesy applies to only one of these three situations—it cannot be used multiple times.

After this courtesy has been used, all future late cancellations/rescheduling or missed appointments will be subject to the standard cancellation policy.

Courtesy exceptions do not apply to late arrivals, or cancellation/rescheduling of consultations without proper notice outlined by our policies.



Due to the nature of our services, we do not offer refunds– with exception for refunding a deposit if an appointment is canceled with more than 48 hours’ notice, or the one-time courtesy exception described above. If you are dissatisfied with any service received, please contact us within one week of your service to discuss your concerns.



NEA Electrolysis does not offer appointment time slots longer than 60 minutes. However, if your electrologist deems it safe and appropriate for your treatment needs, you may schedule multiple appointments in the same day– consecutive or non-consecutive, subject to appointment availability.

There will be a 20 minute break between appointments. Please speak with your electrologist before scheduling multiple appointments in the same day or week.
