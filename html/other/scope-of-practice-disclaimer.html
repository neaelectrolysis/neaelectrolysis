NEA Electrolysis only provides electrolysis for permanent hair removal. Autumn Dildine is a licensed electrologist. In the state of Arkansas, electrologists are generally required to complete 600 hours of formal education & training to practice.

Under Arkansas Cosmetology Law, "Electrologist" is defined as: "Any person who permanently removes hair from or destroys hair on the human body for beautification by the use of an electric needle or by the use of any other kinds of devices or appliances designed to permanently remove hair from the human body."
