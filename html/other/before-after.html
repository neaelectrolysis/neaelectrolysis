Before & After


Age mid 40s
Ethnicity Caucasian
Skin Type Fitspatrick 3
Hours of Treatment Approx 6-8
Months/Year Jan, Feb, March 2026
Modality Blend & Thermolysis
Details Case #1 received a full hysterectomy, including removal of both ovaries, approx 2 years prior to receiving electrolysis. They were prescribed estradiol for HRT following the surgery, but discontinued it shortly after.

It's presumed they developed hirsutism, with estradiol deficiency being the primary driver. They also have high density accelerated hair on cheeks & side burns, indicating these follicles are also being stimulated.

They received Blend and Thermolysis on chin & neck over a period of 3 months, approx 6 to 8 hours of total treatment. They began transdermal estradiol towards the end of March; However it's unlikely to have an impact on visible hairs at this point in time.

*Note: "First Clearance" refers to only terminal hairs. We were not prioritizing the accelerated vellus on chin.
