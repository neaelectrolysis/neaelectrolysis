Modality Blend & Galvanic
Details Model received about 1 hour of Galvanic & Blend on navel. Photos show how treatment area appears afterwards.

Photo #2 shows the treatment area immediately after, while Photo #3 shows the area 1-2 hours later.

The red spots in #3 are "perifollicular erythema", this is a normal side effect and took about 3-4 weeks to resolve. Common causes of erythema is dilated blood vessels and/or increased number of capillaries (tiny blood vessels). The navel may be more prone to slower healing, compared to the face in healthy subjects. Healing is influenced by blood circulation, which varies between body regions.

The visible hair reduction as seen in Photo #3 remained for atleast 3 months following this single treatment, however it was not photographically documented.




Age 48
Ethnicity Caucasian

Hours of Treatment Approx 6-8
Months/Year 12/25-03/26
Modality Blend & Thermolysis
Details Model received a full hysterectomy, including removal of both ovaries, approx 2 years prior to receiving electrolysis. They were prescribed estradiol for HRT following the surgery, but discontinued it shortly after.

It's presumed they developed hirsutism, with estradiol deficiency being the primary cause. They also have high density accelerated vellus hair on cheeks & side burns, indicating these follicles are also being stimulated.

They received Blend and Thermolysis on chin & neck over a period of 3 months, approx 6 to 8 hours of total treatment. They began transdermal estradiol in February; However it's unlikely to have an impact on visible hairs at this point in time.

*Note: "First Clearance" refers to only terminal hairs. We were not prioritizing the accelerated vellus on chin.
