Who is the Name & Face Behind NEA Electrolysis?


My name is Autumn Dildine, and I've proudly called Arkansas my home all 29 years of my life. I'm a licensed electrologist in both Wisconsin and Arkansas, with certifications in Barbicide and Bloodborne Pathogens.

Outside of work, I enjoy dabbling in photography, web design; reading, painting, and spending time with my friends & family. I'm also a big fan of Minecraft, The Sims, and Scifi movies.

I graduated from the Electrology Institute of Wisconsin in November 2025. Since then, I've spent my time building knowledge & traveling out of state to practice on volunteers.

I'm so excited to pursue this career! With my passion for helping others and my own struggles with unwanted hair, this is an amazing opportunity to do my part in building a better world- in an area where interests & skills like mine are needed. I care deeply about promoting self-education, advocacy, and improved standards.

My goal is to provide excellent service, helping others to feel more confident in their own skin; and never forget there is always more to learn. Everyone deserves to love themselves through each chapter of their story, and I'm honored to be even a small part of that journey.
