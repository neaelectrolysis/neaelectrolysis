Exciting Announcement: PCOS changed to "PolyEndocrine Metabolic Syndrome"

05/19/23

I'm very excited for this change. I've been personally affected, and have read so many stories by individuals harmed by ignorant healthcare providers entrusted with diagnosing & treating their PMOS.

While I was diagnosed very early as an adolescent, it took over a decade for me to receive proper care. I hope this change motivates providers to respect the various issues & treatment needs seen in PMOS, with the research that's been ongoing for decades now & what has been built into the 2023 International Evidence Based Guideline for the Assessment and Management of PCOS.

There is no excuse for providers not familiarizing themselves with this guideline. It's not just cysts, or weight gain, or trouble conceiving. While not everyone with PMOS has insulin resistance, it is SO important to manage when it's part of the picture. The damage from delayed diagnosis & treatment can be very serious. We all deserve better.

Check out this article from The Endocrine Society to learn more about the name change and what it means for us!
