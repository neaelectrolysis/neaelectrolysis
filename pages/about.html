Who is the Name & Face Behind NEA Electrolysis LLC?


My name is Autumn Dildine, born & raised in NEA. I recently graduated from the Electrology Institute of Wisconsin, and I'm in the process of obtaining my electrology license for Arkansas. I purchased an LLC for early business operations purposes, but will convert to a PLLC once I'm licensed in AR & ready to offer a professional service.

In my free time, I enjoy dabbling in photography, graphic design, web design; reading, painting, and spending time with my friends & family. I'm so excited to be pursuing a career in electrology! With my passion for helping others, along with my own struggles with unwanted hair, this is an amazing opportunity to do my part in building a better world- in an area where interests & skills like mine is needed & appreciated.

My aspiration is to provide excellent service, spread compassion & kindness; advocate for equality, acceptance, improving standards & quality of care; and never forget there is always more to learn.

Everyone deserves to love themselves through each chapter of their story, and I'm honored to be part of that journey.
