Who is the Name & Face Behind NEA Electrolysis LLC?


My name is Autumn Dildine and I've lived all 29 years of my life in Northeast Arkansas. In my free time, I enjoy dabbling in photography, graphic design, web design; reading, painting, and spending time with my friends & family.

I graduated from the Electrology Institute of Wisconsin in November 2025, and I'm working to obtain my electrology license for Arkansas. I purchased an LLC for early business operations, but will convert to a PLLC once I'm licensed in AR & ready to offer a professional service.

I'm so excited to be pursuing a career in electrology! With my passion for helping others and my own struggles with unwanted hair, this is an amazing opportunity to do my part in building a better world- in an area where interests & skills like mine is needed. I care deeply about promoting self-education, advocacy, and improved standards in professions like electrology.

My goal is to provide excellent service, help others to feel confident in their own skin; and never forget there is always more to learn. Everyone deserves to love themselves through each chapter of their story, and I'm honored to be part of that journey.
