A Little About Me:

My name is Autumn Dildine, I'm 28 years old and was born & raised in Northeast Arkansas. I'm so excited for my upcoming career in electrology; I've always had an interest in science & medicine, and with electrology being adjacent to healthcare & cosmetics, it wasn't hard to choose this path!

I love helping people, and have interests in studying PCOS & related topics. I'm no stranger to the challenges people face with unwanted hair & dysphoria. In my free time, I enjoy various art-related hobbies, reading, cooking, watching scifi movies, spending time with my family, and playing minecraft.

One of my ambitions in life is to do my part in building a world that's more accepting of ourselves & each other. It's my hope that future generations grow up blessed with a stronger set of skills to accept & love themselves, in spite of their differences; what so many of us have spent a large part of our adulthood catching up on.

Always remember, you deserve to love yourself & feel confident through every chapter of your journey!

Electrologist Survey (United States) 2025

In 2025 I released a survey, asking electrologists to share information about their careers, with the objective to collect data to assess current job growth & demand, and help determine whether becoming an electrologist is a good career investment. Besides the results of my survey, the only data I could find on electrology was lumped in with other professions under "Skincare Specialists", on the US Bureau of Labor Statistics website.

Click here if you're an electrologist and wish to participate in the survey! It takes about 10-15 minutes & submissions are anonymous unless you choose to share your email at the end.
