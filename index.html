About the Photographer            


"Every child is an artist.
The problem is how to remain an artist once we grow up."
-Picasso


           My name is Autumn G Dildine and I'm a college
student living in Pocahontas, AR. I'm majoring in
Graphic Design and I aspire to enhance my skills in all
forms of visual art. If I'm not at school working, then
I'm busy with projects like this one. Some of my other
interests include oil painting, rock music, scifi-tv
shows, and web design.

           I've been interested in photography since I
was a young teen. I've known I have a knack for it,
and my iphone selfies were the bomb! I LOVE taking
pictures of the sky, especially sunsets. In early 2018
I decided it was time to pursue this craft and I
bought my first DSLR camera, a decision
I'll never regret!

           My aspiration as an amateur photographer is to
connect with my clients, make them feel as comfortable
as possible, and capture their best! I'm interested in
all subjects, from families to weddings. I currently offer
a variety of sessions; Visit my Services & Prices page
to learn more.
