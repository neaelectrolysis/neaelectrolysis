About Electrolysis

Electrolysis is the only FDA approved method for permanent hair removal. Laser hair removal isn't an option for clients with blond or red hair, as the laser can only target the brown/black pigments in hair. Likewise, electrolysis tends to be more effective than laser for clients with imbalanced hormones, although some clients with imbalanced hormones still report satisfactory results with laser hair removal. There is a greater risk for inducing paradoxical hypertrichosis with laser hair removal if the client has imbalanced hormones, especially on the face area.
