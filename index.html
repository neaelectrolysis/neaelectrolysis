Generally speaking, many electrologists offer time slots for 15-min, 30-min, 45-min, and 60-min appointments. Prices for appointments less than 60 min are generally tiered, to account for the cost of supplies and time spent setting up & cleaning their station. The cost for a 60-min appointment ranges from $85-150, sometimes depending on the area of the body.


NEA Electrolysis is happy to offer service on any area of the body, with respect to laws or safety/liability concerns; However, the service will not be offered for "Brazilian" areas until some time after opening.
