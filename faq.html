General Questions

Q: Where are you located? Do you travel?
I live in Pocahontas, AR. I can shoot anywhere in Randolph County with no extra fee. I'm happy to travel for a session; Travel fees vary per location so get in touch with me for an estimate.

Q: What times & days do you schedule sessions?
If we're shooting outdoors I prefer scheduling between 7:30am-11am and 3pm-5pm so we get the best lighting. If we shoot indoors I will meet anytime that works for my client.

Q: Do you have a reservation fee?
Yes, the reservation fee is required to book a session and is $50 (non-refundable). The fee will be credited towards your session.

Q: What is your reschedule policy?
I appreciate being notified that an appointment needs to be rescheduled atleast 24 hours in advance.

Q: What is included in my session fee?
Your session fee includes the shoot, free photo enhancement, and free print release (for personal use). You can purchase digital copies, various print options, and a copyright release separately.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
I accept cash, Paypal, Venmo, Debit Cards, and Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Discover).

Q: How do I book a session?
Visit my Contact page or send an email to autumngailphotography@gmail.com

Q: How do I order prints?
Visit my Order Prints page or email me if you'd prefer to order through phone or in person.

Post Session

Q: Do I receive the original photos in addition with prints/digital copies?
For purchased prints or digital copies you receive the files I've enhanced. However, I am happy to provide original files upon request.

Q: How many photos make it to the final product of our session?
Essentially, I aim for as many great photos as possible. This varies per session, so if you're curious please get in touch with me!

Q: What is "photo enhancement"? How do you edit your photos?
Photo enhancement is the term I use for the basic edits I do to photos. I love playing with elements of a photo, but for my clients I keep it natural & beautiful. I also touch-up blemishes and acne. If you're interested in more dramatic editing, let me know!

Q: Do you keep a backup of my photos?
I keep a backup of sessions for one year. If you loose purchased photos, I can provide replacements for a $50 replacement fee. During this year, you can also access an online gallery of your photos (granted access with digital/print purchase)

Q: How archival are your prints?
My prints will last up to 100 years in a typical home display, and up to 200 years if they are stored in the dark.

Q: How long do I wait to see the results of our session?
I will finish editing and organizing your photos within 1 to 2 weeks, then I will email you watermark files to review.

Q: How do I access my online personal gallery?
Once you purchase access to the gallery, I will email a link and password to view it whenever you want. If you have any questions email me and I'll help as soon as I can.
