Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What services do you offer?
A: I offer electrolysis hair removal on nearly all areas of the body, and I also offer advanced electrolysis. For hair removal, I only charge based on time, my price is the same no matter what area of the body. For advanced electrolysis, the price differs depending on the service. Click here for more information on Services & Prices.

Q: What do I need to do before a consultation?
A: Clients must complete & submit the online documents I send before consultation. Clients should be clean, wear comfortable clothes- keeping in mind I'll be examining the area they're seeking services for, discuss treatment goals, expectations, medical history, your current skincare routine. Consultations generally take 15-30 minutes to complete.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: I accept cash, debit cards, credit cards.

Q: When is payment due?
A: The consultation fee ($25.00 + tax)is due at the time of booking your consultation, and a 25% deposit is always due at the time of booking a session. If you book your session or consultation through this platform, payment is easy & convenient. If you schedule in-person or over the phone, I'll send you an invoice that must be payed by the end of the day.

Q: How long will it take to see results from electrolysis?
A: This is a very challenging to answer. The rate of improvement varies wildly depending on the area being treated, the underlying cause of the unwanted hair--

Q: If I stop electrolysis, will that ruin my progress?
A: Electrolysis is the only FDA approved PERMANENT hair removal method. When a skilled electrologist performs electrolysis properly, the follicle is dead forever. It will never grow back! Taking a break from electrolysis sessions won't ruin your progress, it will just delay the time it takes to reach your goals.

Q: Policy on client's privacy
A: As a licensed electrologist, it's my duty to protect your PHI (personal health information) and I must follow HIPAA rules. I will never share the contents of your PHI that I have on file, or even the fact that you're my client, unless you submit a written request.

Q: How long does a session take?
A: The length of your session depends on the area being treated, client's pain tolerance

Q: Is electrolysis painful?
A: Yes, electrolysis can be painful. Clients' pain tolerance varies. During consultation, I'll discuss products you can use during sessions to help ease pain/discomfort.

Q: Do you accept health insurance?
A: I currently don't accept health insurance, but that's something I hope to offer in the future!

Q: What days & times can I schedule a session?
A: I'm available for sessions on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, & Sunday from 1pm-6pm. I encourage clients to share what days/times work best for THEM while filling out consultation paperwork, so I can know if there's a greater need to alter my schedule to better accomodate clients.

Q: What do I need to do on or before the day of my session?
A: Clients need to be clean, wear comfortable clothing, well hydrated, and have stopped shaving so the hair is around 1/4 inch length. I recommend clients trim with electric hair clippers + guard before our session, if the hair is longer than 1/2 inch in length.

Q: What do I need to do after my sessions?
A: Clients should avoid taking hot showers/baths, avoid exfoliation, follow aftercare rules. Following electrolysis for hair removal, some mild redness is expected & should resolve within 24 hours. Keep in mind this is not a comprehensive list, I discuss this more in-depth during our consultation, and clients are aways welcome to reach out to me if they have more questions!
